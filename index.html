Pavel Durov

Entrepreneur, programmer, dollar billionaire
  • durov

Programmer and businessman, one of the founders of the VKontakte social network and the company of the same name, the cross-platform Telegram messenger and other projects. The dollar billionaire, whose fortune is estimated at $ 17 billion, is ranked 112th among the world's billionaires according to Forbes magazine. A person who puts at the head of activity not earnings, but the process of creation, which in our time is a huge rarity.

Biography

  • 10th October 1984

    Born in Leningrad, in an intelligent family.

  • 1995 year

    First took a great interest in programming

  • 2001 year

    Graduated with honors from the Academic Gymnasium

  • 2002 year

    Entered the Philological Faculty of St. Petersburg State University with a degree in English Philology and Translation

  • 2006 year

    Graduated from the university with honors

    In the summer, a friend introduced Pavel to facebook, after which Pavel got his first VKontakte concept, called Student.ru.

    On October 1, according to official data, the domain vkontakte.ru was registered

  • 2007 year

    Vkontakte becomes the third most popular website on the Russian Internet

    Durov was recognized as one of the winners in the competition "Best Young Entrepreneurs 2007"

  • 2008 year

    The network was monetized, and the number of users exceeded 20 million

  • 2010 year

    Pavel's company moved into the "Singer" house, which is located on Nevsky Prospekt

  • 2011 year

    Durov, with a fortune of 7.9 billion rubles, ranked 350th in the ranking of Russian billionaires

    Pavel and the head of the DST Global fund Yuri Milner launched the Start Fellows charity project

    The first "appeals" of the FSB
